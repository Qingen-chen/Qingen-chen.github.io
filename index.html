Siberian Husky

Husky is a very common pet dog, it is also known as Siberian Husky.

Husky's biggest advantage is its beautiful appearance.
Almost everyone who has seen Husky will fall in love with Husky's tall figure.

The Siberian Husky is a dog breed that is very close to the wolf's pedigree,
so it looks very like a wolf and has thicker hair than most dog breeds.
